Ingredients for the fettucine al fredo
•	12 ounces fettuccine or other long pasta
•	Kosher salt
•	¼ cup (½ stick) unsalted butter
•	¾ cup finely grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
•	Freshly ground black pepper

Directions
1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2.Add fettuccini and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. In a large saucepan, melt butter into cream over low heat. 
4. Add salt, pepper and garlic salt. ...
5. Add pasta to sauce.
